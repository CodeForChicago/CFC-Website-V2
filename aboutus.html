Vision and Goals

Every child is interested in learning about technology. Every child is curious. But we know all too well that curiosity often wanes as they get older(shucks). Our Goal is to preserve that curiosity by introducing them to a realm of learning where the amount of knowledge is unfathomable and has real-world applications. And it's what all the cool kids are learning! Well, we think we're cool...
